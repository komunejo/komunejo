---
id: REQ-002
entity: requirement
title: A space's integrity is guaranteed deterministically, not by agent discipline
status: open
priority: must
date: 2026-07-12
---

The structured facts of a space — who, what, in which state, related how — must be validated by a deterministic engine, never entrusted to any agent’s memory or discipline. Consistency is claimed only from validator output. This is the space-level restatement of the engine’s own reason for existing ([[DEC-001]]).
