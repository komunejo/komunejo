---
id: REQ-001
entity: requirement
title: The kernel serves any human-agent collaboration, not one domain
status: open
priority: must
date: 2026-07-12
---

The kernel must be usable for any space where humans and their agents collaborate over records — peer review, a personal Zettelkasten, a research log — without carrying any domain’s vocabulary, entities, or operations. Domains arrive as particularizations ([[DEC-008]]); the kernel itself knows none of them.
